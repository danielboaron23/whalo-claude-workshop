A few examples, by team

Three quick illustrations of what Claude can take off people's plates — across different teams and everyday workflows.

Finance & Operations · P2

Legal / compliance agent

Scans updates and new requirements in the gaming industry so our terms of use and privacy policies stay up to date.

  • Shows web research + reading dense policy text
  • Flags what changed and what we may need to update
  • Easy to demo against a real public regulation page
HR · P1

Resume screening assistant

Feed a job description plus a few CVs, get a ranked, scored shortlist with the reasoning behind each score.

  • Universally relatable across teams
  • Runs standalone — no need to touch the live ATS on stage
  • Shows reasoning over messy documents
Product · P1

Daily brief agent

Pull from Slack and Notion into a short morning digest — then schedule it to run every morning on its own.

  • "Focus the daily on what's important"
  • Shows automation + scheduling — the hidden superpower
  • Speaks directly to managers
