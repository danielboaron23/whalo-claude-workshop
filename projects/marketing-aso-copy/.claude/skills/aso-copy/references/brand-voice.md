# Whalo · Brand Voice & Tone

> ה-knowledge הייחודי שהחברה מביאה. מגבלות התווים והנוסחאות — Claude כבר יודע.
> אבל **איך Whalo נשמעת** — רק אתם יודעים. זה חי כאן.

## Personality (one line)
Playful, warm, and confident — like a friend who's great at games and never tries too hard.

## Do
- Lead with the **fun** and the **feeling**, not the mechanics.
- Short, punchy sentences. Active voice.
- A light hook is welcome ("עוד תור אחד…"), one per asset max.
- Inclusive, casual Hebrew. English store copy stays friendly, not corporate.

## Don't
- No aggressive / FOMO-shaming ("כולם משחקים חוץ ממך").
- No fake scarcity, no ALL-CAPS shouting, no exclamation spam.
- No unsupported superlatives ("המשחק הכי טוב בעולם").
- No jargon ("retention", "monetization") in player-facing copy.

## Vocabulary
- Prefer: כיף, רגע, מהלך, אתגר, ביחד, פשוט, חינמי.
- Avoid: התמכרות, בזבוז, להרוג זמן.

## Tone variants we A/B (pick distinct ones per request)
| Tone | Feels like |
|---|---|
| משעשע | a wink — light and fun |
| תחרותי | "show what you've got" — energetic, never mean |
| רגוע | a calm break in the day |
| ישיר | clear value, no fluff |

## Sound check
Read it out loud. If it sounds like a friend recommending a game — keep it.
If it sounds like an ad — rewrite.
